OVERVIEW

"Once you’ve earned his loyalty, there is nothing a dragon won’t do for you." – Hiccup

Set in the Viking village of Berk, How to Train Your Dragon follows Hiccup, an outcast who discovers that dragons are not the dangerous creatures his tribe believes them to be. Through his bond with Toothless, he changes the way Vikings and dragons coexist.

The film explores themes of friendship, acceptance, and bravery. As Hiccup trains Toothless, he learns that understanding and compassion are more powerful than fear and violence. How to Train Your Dragon is a heartfelt journey of discovery, not just for Hiccup, but for the entire village of Berk.
