CHARACTERS

Hiccup Horrendous Haddock III

The main protagonist, a young Viking who befriends a dragon.

Toothless

A Night Fury dragon and Hiccup’s loyal companion.

Astrid Hofferson

A brave Viking and Hiccup’s close friend/love interest.

Stoick the Vast

Hiccup’s father and chief of Berk.

Gobber the Belch

The village blacksmith and Hiccup’s mentor.

Ruffnut & Tuffnut

Viking twins known for their mischievous antics.

Fishlegs Ingerman

A Viking with deep knowledge of dragons.

Snotlout Jorgenson

A cocky Viking warrior and Hiccup’s rival-turned-friend.
